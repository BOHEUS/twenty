---
title: Can I disable importing internal emails?
description: Information about option regarding internal emails in Twenty.
---

## Current Status

Yes, it's possible to disable importing internal emails to Twenty.

## How to disable importing internal emails

1. Open Settings
2. Toggle Advanced on
3. Go to Security
4. At the bottom of the page in Other category there is Sync Internal Emails option

## Important notes

By default, this setting is off. Imported internal emails before the change won't be removed from Twenty.
